Tons of Activities

FROM SPORTS TO SIGHTSEEING, WE HAVE IT ALL

Home to several white sand beaches, waterfalls, hiking trails, and a wide variety of outdoor activities, there is so much to see and do in Puerto Galera! If you have a specific activity in mind or need help with your itinerary, the staff at Amihan Del Sol are more than willing to help you plan for the kind of vacation that you want.

Island Hopping Destinations

There is no shortage of breathtaking beaches in Puerto Galera - with its fine sand, crystal blue waters, and marine life, it comes as no surprise that island hopping and snorkelling are a couple of the must-do’s when in the area. Whether you opt to book a tour or hire a boat and go exploring on your own, you will not be disappointed with these natural gems. A motorized bangka can bring you to nearby Bayanan Beach, Haligi Beach, Sandbar, and Maniknik Beach. The smaller bangkas can bring you to Giant Clam, the Underwater Cave, and to Coral Garden.

Inland Tour Destinations

If you’d like to veer away from the beach and stay inland, then there are several things for you to do, as well! Hike up to and enjoy either the majestic Tamaraw Falls, Talipanan Falls, or Tukuran Falls. Getting to these may be a bit tricky as they may require a jeepney and tricycle ride, before setting off onto their trails (so make sure you ask for directions from Amihan Del Sol’s friendly staff).

You can also opt to visit Mangayan Village, home to the Irayas, which is an indigenous tribe characterized by their dark complexion and dark, curly hair. Believed to have traded with Chinese merchants as early as 1250 AD, the Irayas are proud to have been able to maintain their traditional practices and weave handicrafts which can be bought in several stores across the country.

Water Sports

Amihan Del Sol can also arrange a number of water activities for the adrenalin junkies. Best enjoyed in groups.
