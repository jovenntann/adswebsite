Amihan Del Sol

The Wind, The Sun and The Sea

‘Amihan’ is the Filipino term for the Northeastern Monsoon, the winds that blow during the cool months. ‘Del Sol’ is the Spanish vernacular for: “of the sun.” Refreshing winds greet you as soon as you enter the vicinity. Our modern architecture is a showcase of light and air, with high ceilings, glass windows and sunlit rooms. The real treat, of course, comes when you step out onto the balcony and behold the majestic view of the emerald mountainside and the sparkling sapphire sea.

Upon check-in, guests are treated to refreshing welcome drinks before being led to their rooms. Edison bulbs, refurbished wood, and handmade steampunk furnishings run throughout the property.

The warm hospitality that Filipinos are known for, guests are sure to enjoy their stay. Whether it be a romantic getaway, a solitary retreat, or an action-packed adventure, there’s something for everyone at Amihan del Sol. With over 7,000 islands, the Philippines has unsurprisingly become a tourist favorite - especially with Puerto Galera as one of the country’s leading destinations. Amihan Del Sol offers guests an exquisite jump off point to explore this Filipino province brimming with white sand beaches, excellent snorkelling and diving spots, unexplored mountain ranges, and more.

LUXURY HOTEL

Overlooking Aninuan Beach and the Oriental Mindoro is Amihan del Sol, the perfect getaway for travelers looking to enjoy the best of Puerto Galera. The newly-renovated hilltop property spans over 4000 square meters, has 13 tastefully-designed rooms, two pools, and offers guests breathtaking views of the island’s lush greenery, turquoise waters, and blue skies.
