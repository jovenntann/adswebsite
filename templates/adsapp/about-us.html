Amihan Del Sol

The Wind, The Sun and The Sea

‘Amihan’ is the Filipino term for the Northeastern Monsoon, the winds that blow during the cool months. ‘Del Sol’ is the Spanish vernacular for: “of the sun.” Refreshing winds greet you as soon as you enter the vicinity. Our modern architecture is a showcase of light and air, with high ceilings, glass windows and sunlit rooms. The real treat, of course, comes when you step out onto the balcony and behold the majestic view of the emerald mountainside and the sparkling sapphire sea.
